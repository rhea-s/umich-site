Work

I am a sophomore studying Computer Science Engineering at the University of Michigan.
I conducted undergraduate research under Dr. Walter Lasecki as part of his CroMa Lab here at Michigan. We worked with the goal of creating a library of affordance templates that robots could use to identify objects surrounding them in order to more naturally, efficiently, and accurately interact with humans.

I am also in the Controls division of the Michigan Hyperloop team, which creates and tests a prototype pod in SpaceX's Hyperloop competition. I think Hyperloop has huge potential to revolutionalize our transportation systems which have remained stagnant for too long.

I believe in the importance of actively participating and speaking up on current events and social issues, so I write for the news section of the Michigan Daily .

I'm always on the search for new opportunities to develop my skills. Feel free to contact me if you would like me to work on a project for you!
